About AGAM

Agam, whose name means "inner soul" in Tamil, has captivated audiences worldwide with a genre-defying sound that weaves intricate Carnatic ragas into complex progressive rock arrangements. With celebrated tracks like “Malhar Jam” featured on MTV Coke Studio and an impressive history of sold-out tours across the United States, Agam stands at the forefront of India's contribution to the global fusion music movement.
